MISSION

HUNTER has been one of the promising firms in the global shipping industry over the past few years. We are a focused, skilled, and resourceful team of players who believe perfection and performance is our key concern. We provide quality ship repairs & services.

We do this with great competitiveness, professionalism, and time-bound commitment. What makes us different is our unique style of absorbing client requirements, safety concerns, standards, and tackling the same with the best industrially possible measures and solutions.

Looking ahead, the team will build on past success and wishes to enhance its reputation as a leading ship repair service provider, offering marine services to a wide client base in operation.

VISION

“To become an indispensable service provider in the marine sector, offering one-stop marine solutions for all vessels operating in the region with uncompromising quality and safety”.
